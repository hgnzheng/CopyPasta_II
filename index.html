Abstract

In an era where biosignal monitoring is essential yet current analysis tools remain overly complex, our platform offers an intuitive, interactive solution. It empowers doctors and patients to quickly identify crisis points in biosignals and simulate potential interventions. With features that allow comparing all tracks from the same device and the option to isolate a single track for detailed simulation when track data is overwhelming, our tool transforms complex biosignal data into actionable insights for improved patient care.

Background

Despite significant advances in biosignal monitoring, clinicians still face steep learning curves with traditional analysis software. Many existing tools lack real-time interactivity and are not designed to handle the high volume of data generated by modern devices. This creates challenges in quickly interpreting critical health signals and making timely decisions. Our platform addresses these limitations by providing a user-friendly interface that enables comprehensive comparisons across all device tracks, while also offering the flexibility to focus on individual tracks for simulation. This dual approach simplifies data interpretation, enhances diagnostic efficiency, and ultimately leads to more proactive and informed patient care.

The Big Picture

Summary

Our dashboard not only captures the intricate rhythms of surgical procedures but also transforms this data into actionable insights. By integrating comprehensive biosignal recordings with interactive analysis tools, we enable medical professionals to visualize, compare, and simulate critical intervention scenarios in real time.

Understanding the Dataset

Our visualization is built upon a comprehensive dataset from a Korean hospital, capturing vital biosignals during surgical procedures. Each case contains minute-by-minute recordings of heart rate, blood pressure, oxygen saturation, and other critical measurements, alongside relevant lab results. This robust dataset forms the foundation for our interactive simulations and track comparisons.

The Life of a Surgery

Just as a human has a pulse, a surgery has a rhythm—moments of stability, critical turning points, and interventions that redirect its course. Our dashboard captures this "surgery pulse," allowing medical professionals to visualize, analyze, and learn from these critical rhythms.

Every surgery tells a story: from the initial incision through critical moments to the final suture. By visualizing these narratives, we enable a deeper understanding of surgical dynamics and better preparation for future procedures.

Crisis Analysis

Summary

Building on the interactive dashboard's real-time detection of crisis points, our Crisis Analysis module elevates biosignal monitoring by transforming complex data into clear, actionable insights. It helps healthcare professionals quickly identify critical events and anomalies, ensuring that no pivotal moment goes unnoticed.

  • Comparative Analysis

    Easily compare all biosignal tracks from the same device on a single graph, or isolate a specific track for a focused simulation. This flexibility is essential for managing large datasets, revealing significant trends and anomalies that inform decision-making.

  • Interactive Simulation

    Empowering users to simulate potential interventions, our module projects how biosignals might evolve following targeted actions. This predictive capability offers a clear look at how such interventions could improve patient outcomes.

Project Documentation

The Surgery's Pulse dashboard visualizes real-time biosensor data from surgical procedures, allowing physicians to track vital signs, detect anomalies, and simulate interventions for better decision-making during critical moments.

Key features include:

  • Animated Time-Series Visualization: Track vital signs with playback controls.
  • Overview Timeline: Navigate through the entire surgery using brushing and linking.
  • Anomaly Detection: Automatically identify and highlight critical events.
  • Crisis Analysis: Deep-dive into critical moments with simulation capabilities.

Click on any anomaly marker or event annotation to enter the detailed crisis analysis view.

Technical Implementation

This dashboard is built using D3.js for visualization, with data processing handled through a PHP and Python backend. The system processes large datasets progressively to maintain performance while providing high-resolution visualizations.

Data sources include:

  • Biosignal time-series (heart rate, blood pressure, etc.)
  • Lab results with timestamps
  • Case metadata (procedure type, complexity, timestamps)

Getting Started

New users can quickly begin by accessing the interactive dashboard and filtering cases based on operation type, complexity, and case ID. For a step-by-step guide, please refer to the Interactive Dashboard section and the Demo Video .
